There is an infinite number of chakras in the human body, but mostly we hear about the ‘seven chakras’. These can be named as root chakra, sacral chakra, solar plexus chakra, hear chakra, throat chakra, third eye chakra and crown chakra. A specific color, element, stone and yoga pose is associated with each chakra. Depending on whether these energy centres are healthy or blocked, you may experience different attributes of your physical, mental, and emotional health.

Root Chakra (Muladhara)

"I can't grow from an unsteady foundation."

Represents our foundation. On the human body, it sits at the base of the spine and gives us the feeling of being grounded. When the root chakra is open, we feel confident in our ability to withstand challenges and stand on our own two feet. When it's blocked, we feel threatened, as if we're standing on unstable ground.

Sacral Chakra (Muladhara)

"I always honor others but not before myself."

Helps inform how we relate to our emotions and emotions of others. It also governs creativity and sexual energy. Those with a blocked sacral chakra could feel a lack of control in their lives.

Solar Plexus Chakra (Manipura)

"Self-love starts when I accept all parts of myself."

Speaks to your ability to be confident and in control of your life. If your solar plexus chakra is blocked, you might feel overwhelming amounts of shame and self-doubt. Those with open sacral chakras are free to express their true selves.

Location Color Element Stone Yoga pose
Lower abdomen Orange Fire Amber Boat pose

Heart Chakra (Anahata)

"When I love myself, loving others comes easily."

Bridge between the lower chakras and the upper chakras. This chakra can influence our ability to give and receive love from others and ourselves. Someone with a blocked heart chakra will have difficulty fully opening up to the people in their life. If someone's heart is open, they can experience deep compassion and empathy.

Location Color Element Stone Yoga pose
Center of chest Green Air Rose quartz Camel pose

Throat Chakra (Vishuddha)

"I speak my truth, always."

Gives voice to the heart chakra and controls our ability to communicate our personal power. When it's functioning at full capacity, it allows us to express ourselves truly and clearly. Someone with a blocked throat chakra will feel like they have trouble finding the words to say how they truly feel.

Third-eye Chakra (Ajna)

"I am open to exploring what cannot be seen."

Controls our ability to see the big picture and connect to intuition. Visions and intuitive hits are not uncommon for someone with an open third-eye chakra.

Location Color Element Stone Yoga pose
Forehead between eyes Dark blue/purple Light Amethyst Child pose

Crown Chakra (Sahasrara)

"I am a vessel for love and light."

Represents our ability to be fully connected spiritually. When you fully open your crown chakra—something very few people ever do!—you're able to access a higher consciousness.
